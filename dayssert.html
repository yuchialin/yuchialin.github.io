About the Project

In a team of five, we helped a local bakery store research, design and build their eCommerce Website for our final Front-End Developers bootcamp project.

Goals

We interviewed our stakeholder to better understand their goals of the website to make sure that our design addressed the needs.

Branding

Clean and modern design that will attract Dayssert's target users.

Usability

Provide a smooth and quick online shopping experience for users.

Informative

Inform users about the origins of the ingredients and the process of producing them.

Final Design

Design Process

Competitive Domain Research Research Finding Persona Site Maps & Functional Map UI Flow Wireframe Style Guide

Competitive Domain Research

In order to understand the needs of the dessert market, we conducted several competitive domain research for recipes websites, such as cookpad and U-chef. We listed out the pros and cons of each website and the potential difficulties that users may face while navigating through those sites.

Research Finding

  • Customers love sharing their baking results with people who have similar interests. Therefore, having an interactive comment section will allow users to revisit the site more often after they purchase the product.
  • Sometime it can be difficult to locate the desired recipe due to the large amount of information and the poorly designed information architecture.

Persona

Based on the goals of the website and the research finding, we created persona, so we can keep the users in mind while designing.

Site Maps & Functional Map

Based on research findings, we created a site map and a functional maps to meets client's needs and the users needs.

User Flow

To ensure the shopping process is quick and smooth, we created user flows before start developing the website. Through creating user flows, we could easily visualize how many steps did the users have to take to complete their tasks.

Style Guide

We used dark blue as the primary color and gold as the accent color. We chose white for the background so it wouldn't steal the spotlight from dessert. The combination of these threes elements creates an elegant atmosphere.
