The Opportunity Hub

How to increase the students' engagement rate with the Hub?

Role

UX Researcher

Duration

Sep. 2018 - Dec. 2018 (3 Months)

Team

Quin DeVries, Yiwei Xu, Max Zhang

Tools

Contextual Inquiry, Affinity Wall

About the Project

As a team of five, we are invited to do an investigation on the current use of the Opportunity Network System and see how to increase students' engagements with it. A lot of students log in to the the network, but few students actually RSVP to attend the events and brows around the network.

User Research

Contextual Inquiry

Based on the research finding, we defined the relevant interviewees, including 4 staff and 4 students, and developed the interview protocol accordingly. We also did a interpretation session after each interview. One person took note and other members listen and wrote down their insights.
