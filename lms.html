THE PROBLEM

After introducing the LMS to the enterprise, the Human Resource (HR) Department usually encountered a problem - the low traffic rate of using the platform. Despite the fact that the HR spent lots of effort and time creating and uploading learning materials, the employees were still unwilling to engage with the platform.

SOLUTION

During the first three months at the Learning Digital, I helped optimize features for our Learning Management System (LMS). Through enhancing features and adding gamification elements to the platform, user engagement rate increased by 15%.

User Research

USER INTERVIEW

I conducted interviews with two people from HR Department and three regular employees to gain insight on the low engagement rate reason.

KEY INSIGHT

  • the tedious contents of the learning material and the lack of incentives and rewards for learning
  • busy daily workload.

Design & Result

After conducting the research, I optimized two features to stimulate the usage.

1. POINTS EXCHANGE

Our system already have a reward system that allows Human Resource to reward points to employees when employees perform certain activities on the platform. Yet those points meant nothing but bragging right. By adding the point exchange feature, employees can turn their points into physical rewards.

2. MICRO COURSE

In order to build learning materials for the courses, the HR Department needed to spend lots of effort creating the content; the courses usually took employees at least two hours to complete. Therefore, we established a new feature, micro course, for HR to design much more interactive and customized courses that are generally shorter. With those mircro courses, the employees can now better utilize their fragment time.
