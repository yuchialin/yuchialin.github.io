THE CLIENT

The opportunity Hub is a College initiative that aims to help students in College of Literature, Science, and the Arts (LSA) to embrace the valuable skills they learn at the coursework and discover how those skills are related to their broader goals as individuals and how can they further apply those skills to the real-life career after graduation. The Opportunity Hub provides four different services to students to reach this mission, including internships, employer engagement, coaching and prep, alumni engagement.

THE PROBLEM

The Opportunity Hub promotes their events, internships and job opportunities to LSA students through an online platform named LSA Opportunity Network. Students usually enter the platform through the links in the emails or the newsletters that send by the Opportunity Hub. The open rate for those emails is high, but the click rate is relatively low. In addition, they have a lot of students logging in to the platform, but few students actually RSVP to attend the events. The client is requesting us to do an investigation on the current use of the system and how to increase students' engagements with the platform.
